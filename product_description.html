DESCRIPTION:

Satisfying and savory stir fried dark greens with tender stems, served with its classic accompaniment of oyster sauce

INGREDIENTS:

Gai Lan, Garlic, Rice Wine, Fish Sauce, Chicken Stock, Oyster Sauce (Oyster, Salt, Sugar, Modified Cornstarch, Wheat Flour, Caramel Color), Salt Sugar, Canola Oil, Salt, Sugar

INSTRUCTIONS:

7 mins

Cook gai lan and minced garlic with oil on high heat for 30 seconds. Add rice wine and cook until wine is evaporated. Reduce heat to medium, add fish and chicken sauces, and cook for 5 minutes or until tender. Drizzle with oyster sauce.

WARNING

Product contains shellfish, fish, and wheat.
