Slanted Door emerged as a ground-breaking restaurant in San Francisco’s Mission District in 1995. Since then, Chef and owner Charles Phan has fostered a restaurant group that supports and showcases the diverse cultures, values, and passions of the people that comprise the group’s family with locations in San Francisco, Berkeley, San Ramon, Napa, Larkspur, and Las Vegas.

CHARLES PHAN

Charles Phan is the executive chef and owner of Slanted Door family of restaurants, and author of the award-winning cookbooks, Vietnamese Home Cooking and Slanted Door. He opened the original location of the Slanted Door on Valencia Street in San Francisco’s Mission district in 1995, which eventually blossomed into the flagship restaurant at the historic Ferry Building in San Francisco in addition to exciting new projects spanning from throughout the west. Charles’ restaurants and charity work manifest his deep commitment to the art of food as an expression of history, sustainability, and reflection of the changing world and self. He received the James Beard Award for Best Chef California in 2004. In 2011, Charles was inducted into the James Beard Foundation’s Who’s Who of Food in America, and in 2014, Slanted Door won the James Beard award for Outstanding Restaurant. He lives in San Francisco with his wife and dog.

MICHELLE MAH

Michelle Mah is the Director of Operations of Slanted Door family of restaurants. Michelle’s passion for cooking began during her childhood with her family, who emigrated to San Dimas California from Seoul Korea when she was 3. After attaining degrees from University of California San Diego and California Culinary Academy, she worked in some of the Bay Area’s most esteemed restaurants including Jianna, Belon, and Grand Cafe. In 2005, Michelle became the Chef of Kimpton Group’s Ponzu restaurant and was named one of San Francisco Chronicle's "Rising Star Chefs" of 2006. She went on to lead Midi in the Galleria Park Hotel, and the Brixton. Michelle first joined Slanted Door Group in 2011 as the Chef de Cuisine of Wo Hing General Store, went on to helm the flagship Slanted Door restaurant in the Ferry Building, and now oversees high level operations for the Slanted Door Group.
